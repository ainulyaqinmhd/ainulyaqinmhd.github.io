Virtual Embodiment Tour Guide Based on WEB-AR Technology

Project information

  • Category: App design
  • Project date: January 10, 2020
  • Project URL: IGU Travel

This innovative service design utilizes Web-AR technology to enhance backpackers' travel experiences in Taiwan. Web-AR enables the integration of virtual and physical elements, allowing users to access additional information and immersive experiences by scanning objects or locations with their smartphones. With this technology, backpackers can access historical and cultural information, view 3D landmarks, and interact with virtual guides. By leveraging Web-AR, the proposed service design offers a more engaging and interactive travel experience, while promoting and preserving Taiwan's cultural heritage. This project demonstrates the potential of emerging technologies in enhancing travel enjoyment and emphasizes the significance of sustainable and responsible tourism.
